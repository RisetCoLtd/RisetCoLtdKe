Department Of Computer Studies

Computer Department

The computer department is fully equipped with modern computer systems that enable students to get up to date information and technological skills that cut across all the departments.
In addition to basic computer studies, the department offers professional courses.The courses are aimed at equipping the trainees with the necessary knowledge and skills which will enable them work efficiently and effectively in the areas of system analysis, system design and business administrative / Management duties.
At the end of the course units the trainees should be able to:

  1. Operate and use computers and other peripheral devices
  2. Acquire knowledge and skills in software design and development
  3. Appreciate the role of IT in social and economic development
  4. Apply entrepreneurial skills in IT and business environment
  5. Use computer software to solve managerial problems.

Courses under this Department are:

  • Certificate in ICT Level 1
  • Certificate in Computerized Secretarial
  • Certificate in Computerized Accounting
  • Certificate in Graphic Designing
  • Certificate in Video Editing
  • Certificate in Garment Branding
