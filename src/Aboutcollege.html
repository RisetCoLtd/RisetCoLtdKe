Overview of Riset Institute of Technology

Riset Institute of Technology is a tertiary institution known for producing high flying professionals in various disciplines. The institute offers certificates in Community Development and Social Work, Computing and Information Communication Technology, Business Management and related courses.
Each and every course is enriched with in depth practical sessions, short attachment periods of between 2 Weeks and 3 months, regular and extensive academic tours, and at least one foreign language and basic computer skills.
The student alumni of the institute, whose members are holding key positions in different fields, forms a strong network that boost chances of employment for our fresh graduates. Riset Institute accords our top performers first priority in employment opportunities.
High school graduates looking forward to a career with a bright future are greatly encouraged to sample courses offered at Riset Institute of Technology.

Our History

Riset Institute of Technology was founded in the year 2018. It started with 13 students who were taking Computerized Secretarial course only.
The main courses offered were Application Packages and Computerized Secretarial, and Front Office Operations.
As the popularity of the institute grew, the demand for more courses came up. In year 2019 more courses were introduced. These included Business courses; Computerized Accounting courses; and Information Technology courses.
Since then Riset Institute has remained focused on providing students with practical/technical skills as dictated by market demands. This explains why our graduates are on very high demand.
