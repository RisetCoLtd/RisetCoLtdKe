Certificate in ICT Level 1

COURSE OBJECTIVES

The course is intended to:

  1. Impact technical skills to enable students meet the requirement for industry and business today for operators and technical support expert in information communication technology.
  2. Provide a flexible modular alternative Certificate course in information communication technology to take advantage of the varied IT market.
  3. Enable student to progress academically to higher levels of education into relevant Diploma / Degree courses.
  4. Equip students with problem solving to a fast changing technological world.

COURSE CONTENTS:

  • Application Packages
  • Computerized Secretarial
  • Digital Marketing & Networking
  • Quickbooks Accounting
  • Sage Accounting
  • Corel Draw Graphics
  • Photoshop Graphics
  • Internet & Social Media

CAREER PROSPECTS:

Graduates of this course look forward to a career as a Receptionist / Secretary, Front Office managers, or network administrator across both IT and non-IT industries. Depending on specialization, one can also start his/her very own IT business, be a digital forensic analyst, business intelligent analyst, IT security specialist, or sales and marketing executive.
