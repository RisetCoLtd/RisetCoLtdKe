Welcome to Riset Institute of Technology

Riset Institute is a computer training center located in Likoni Ujamaa Area, Mombasa.
We are dedicated to Moulding the Future of our students in ICT Training.
We have developed learning materials and practical exercises which enable our students to get the best outcome in terms of Real World Application of their study courses.

Message from Director

Mr. James Wambua

Riset Institute is ranked the best computer training center within the South coast. Our students usually get employed after completion of their studies or absorbed to higher learning Institutions.
