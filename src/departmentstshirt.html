Department Of Garment Print & Branding

Branding Department

The Branding department is fully equipped with modern Branding Equipments that enable students to get up to date Practicals and proffesional Designs for our Clients.
In addition to Advanced Branding, we offer plain Tshirts and Branding services for personal, Institutional and Cooporate establishments.
The courses are aimed at equipping the trainees with the necessary knowledge and skills which will enable them work efficiently and effectively in the areas of Designing, Large Format Machine management and client relations / Management duties.

Services under this department are:-

  • Plain Tshirts
  • Screen Printing
  • Personal Garment Branding
  • Institutional / Cooporate Garment Branding
  • School Badges, Tracksuits, Sweaters Etc.
  • All Online & Cyber Services
