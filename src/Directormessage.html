DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

MR. JAMES WAMBUA

Whether one is a school leaver, mature student, or employer, it is a great time to apply to us.
Riset Institute of Technology is exciting in a variety of ways. Our students have a High rate of success.
After they finish their academic programmes, most of them either secure a place at universities or get well paying jobs while others excel in the world of entrepreneurship.
The courses that we offer are designed to give students both the knowledge and skills to help them secure a better future.
We have practical training facilities and materials where students gain on site experience.
We ensure that our students are well moulded in terms of confidence, positive attitude and personality.
Our system ensures that soft skills are valued too alongside academic excellence.
Our student experience is officially one of the best in the country.
We recognize that a great institute is about creativity, it is about engagement in a broad co-curricular activities and it is about having fun.
We therefore welcome each and everyone to visit us and witness this wonderful Training Institute located in Mombasa County, Likoni Sub county Ujamaa Area.
